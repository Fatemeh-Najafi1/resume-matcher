Position: Senior Machine Learning Engineer – Healthcare Analytics

Location: Remote / San Francisco, CA

About the Role
We are seeking a highly experienced Machine Learning Engineer to join our healthcare analytics division. The successful candidate will design, train, and deploy large-scale models for clinical text and patient data analysis. This position requires deep expertise in Python, TensorFlow or PyTorch, and modern NLP frameworks.

Responsibilities
- Build, train, and optimize deep learning models for healthcare and medical data.
- Collaborate with data engineers and clinicians to define problem statements and ensure data integrity.
- Develop scalable data pipelines for both structured and unstructured datasets.
- Implement model explainability and responsible AI practices.
- Contribute to the architecture of production ML systems (Docker, Kubernetes, REST APIs).
- Support model monitoring, retraining, and versioning using MLflow or equivalent tools.
- Document experiments, results, and present findings to stakeholders.

Requirements
- 6+ years of experience in machine learning, data science, or AI product development.
- Strong proficiency in Python, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch.
- Experience with NLP (Transformers, BERT, spaCy) and text classification.
- Proficiency in SQL and data wrangling using Pandas or Spark.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and CI/CD pipelines.
- Proven experience in deploying ML models to production environments.
- Excellent communication and cross-functional collaboration skills.
- Master’s degree or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field.

Preferred Qualifications
- Experience with healthcare data (EHRs, HL7, FHIR) and HIPAA compliance.
- Familiarity with explainable AI, model fairness, and ethics.
- Publications or open-source contributions in the ML/NLP domain.

Compensation & Benefits
- Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
- Health, dental, and vision coverage.
- Remote-first team culture with flexible hours.
- Generous learning and conference budget.

Company Overview
MedAI Analytics is a rapidly growing startup at the intersection of healthcare and artificial intelligence. We partner with hospitals and research institutions to deliver data-driven insights that improve patient outcomes. Our mission is to make medical decision support systems accurate, transparent, and equitable.
